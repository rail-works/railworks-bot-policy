Rail.Works Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Rail.Works Bot ("Bot") may access, process, and store when used in Discord servers.

1. Information Collected

The Bot may process the following information as required for its functionality:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord usernames and display names
  • Server IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Message content only when necessary for ticket transcripts or moderation features
  • Role IDs and permissions needed for bot features

2. How Information Is Used

This information is used solely to provide bot features such as:

  • Ticket creation and management
  • Ticket transcripts
  • Moderation and support workflows
  • Role and permission checks

3. Data Storage

The Bot may temporarily or persistently store limited operational data such as ticket counters, ticket ownership mappings, and transcripts where required for functionality.

4. Data Sharing

The Bot does not sell personal data. Data is not shared with third parties except where required for the Bot’s operation within Discord or where required by law.

5. Data Retention

Operational data is retained only as long as necessary for the Bot’s features. Ticket transcripts and logs may be retained for moderation and support purposes at the discretion of server staff.

6. User Rights

If you would like to request deletion of data associated with your use of the Bot, please contact the Rail.Works server staff or support team.

7. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored data, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after updates are made constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For support or privacy-related questions, please contact the Rail.Works server staff or support team.
