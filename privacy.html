Rail.Works Bot - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Rail.Works Discord bot (“Bot”) may process when used in a Discord server.

1. Information We May Process

The Bot may process basic Discord-related data required for its functionality, such as:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server IDs
  • Discord channel IDs
  • Message content where necessary for bot features (such as tickets, moderation logs, or reports)
  • Command usage and interaction data

2. Why We Process This Information

This information is used solely to provide the Bot’s features, including server utilities, moderation tools, ticket systems, support workflows, and logging.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data processed by the Bot is only used for the operation of the Bot and may be visible to authorized server staff depending on how the Bot is configured.

4. Data Retention

Data may be stored only as long as necessary for the Bot’s functionality, moderation purposes, logs, transcripts, or configuration requirements. Some data may be deleted automatically depending on feature design.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data processed by the Bot, but no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Your Rights

If you have concerns about data processed by the Bot, please contact the Rail.Works team through the appropriate support channels. Requests may be handled depending on technical feasibility and operational requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.
