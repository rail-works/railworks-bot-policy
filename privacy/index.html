Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Rail.Works Discord bot (“Bot”) may process when used in a Discord server and how that information is handled.

1. Information We May Process

The Bot may process limited Discord-related data necessary for functionality, including:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server IDs
  • Discord channel IDs
  • Message content where required for features such as tickets, moderation logs, reports, support systems, or transcripts
  • Command usage and interaction data
  • Configuration data needed for server-specific bot features

2. Why We Process This Information

This data is processed solely to provide the Bot’s features, including moderation tools, ticket systems, support workflows, logs, administrative utilities, anti-abuse systems, and server-specific configuration.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data processed by the Bot is only used for the operation of the Bot and may be visible to authorized server staff depending on how the Bot is configured and what features are enabled.

4. Data Retention

Data may be stored only for as long as reasonably necessary for functionality, moderation, logging, transcripts, configuration, debugging, or abuse prevention. Some data may be deleted automatically depending on how features are designed or configured.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data processed by the Bot. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and use of the Bot is at your own risk.

6. Server Administrator Responsibility

Server owners and administrators are responsible for how they configure the Bot, including permissions, access to logs, transcript visibility, moderation features, and any stored outputs generated within their server.

7. Your Rights

If you have concerns about data processed by the Bot, please contact the Rail.Works team through the appropriate support channels. Requests may be reviewed based on technical feasibility, operational necessity, and legitimate moderation or security needs.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
